ntoskrnl-rs in the browser · ntemu

A bespoke x86-64 emulator (~60 KB WebAssembly — no threads, no COOP/COEP) booting the real NT-architecture kernel to an interactive cmd.exe. Click Boot, wait for the C:\> prompt, then type.
